Character Profile

The chief miko of the Narukami Grand Shrine, a descendant of the Hakushin bloodline, a servant and friend of Immortality, and the editor-in-chief of Yae Publishing House, a publisher of creepy short novels... By the way, Yae Miko's nicknames are as numerous as her mood swings. The number of people who have tried to "get to know her" for various reasons could fill the streets from the Tenryou Commission compound to Yae Publishing House if you were to line them all up, but few have succeeded to this day. Not that the miko meant to hide anything. Any disorganization was simply the result of what she wanted and desired. A puzzle without a solution is no puzzle at all!

Gameplay

Constellation

C1 Each time Great Secret Art: Tenko Kenshin activates a Tenko Thunderbolt, Yae Miko will restore 8 Elemental Energy for herself.

C2 Sesshou Sakura start at Level 2 when created, their max level is increased to 4, and their attack range is increased by 60%.

C3 Increases the Level of Yakan Evocation: Sesshou Sakura by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

C4 When Sesshou Sakura lightning hits opponents, the Electro DMG Bonus of all nearby party members is increased by 20% for 5s.

C5 Increases the Level of Great Secret Art: Tenko Kenshin by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

C6 The Sesshou Sakura's attacks will ignore 60% of the opponent's DEF.
